Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

2: The Book of the Twos

104

  • DG SC © Translated from the Pali by Bhikkhu Bodhi  (More copyright information)

1"Bhikkhus, there are these two kinds of fools. What two? One who perceives what is non-Dhamma as Dhamma and one who perceives what is Dhamma as non-Dhamma. These are the two kinds of fools."

1"Dveme, bhikkhave, paṇḍitā. Katame dve? Yo ca dhamme dhammasaññī, yo ca adhamme adhammasaññī. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve paṇḍitā"ti.
