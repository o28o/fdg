2: The Book of the Twos

200–204

  • © Translated from the Pali by Bhikkhu Bodhi  (More copyright information)

1"Bhikkhus, these two qualities lead to the decline of a bhikkhu who is a trainee. What two? (200) Anger and hostility … (201) Denigration and insolence … (202) Envy and miserliness … (203) Deceitfulness and craftiness … (204) Moral shamelessness and moral recklessness. These two qualities lead to the decline of a bhikkhu who is a trainee."
