Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Connected Discourses

SN50: Connected Discourses on the Powers

SN50:35–44 Searches, Etc.

  • dg sc © Translated from the Pali by Bhikkhu Bodhi.  (More copyright information)

(To be elaborated parallel to 45.161–70.)

2Searches, discriminations, taints,
Kinds of existence, threefold suffering,
Barrenness, stains, and troubles,
Feelings, craving, and thirst.

1(Esanāvaggo vitthāretabbo.)

2Esanā vidhā āsavo,
Bhavo ca dukkhatā tisso;
Khilaṁ malañca nīgho ca,
Vedanā taṇhā tasinā cāti.
