Vinaya Piṭaka - Monastic Rules

Bhikkhu Khandhaka

The Khandhaka is often arranged into two parts - The Mahāvagga (Khandhakas 1–10) and the Cullavagga (Khandhakas 11–22).

This arrangement seems to be largely one of convenience so that there are two volumes of manageable size rather than a single huge volume. Both arrangements are included below.

(Mahāvagga)

(Mv1.)     Khandhaka 1Going Forth
(Mv2.)     Khandhaka 2 The Sabbath
(Mv3.)     Khandhaka 3 Rains Season
(Mv4.)     Khandhaka 4 Invitation
(Mv5.)     Khandhaka 5 Leather
(Mv6.)     Khandhaka 6 Medicine
(Mv7.)     Khandhaka 7 Robe-Making Season
(Mv8.)     Khandhaka 8 Robes
(Mv9.)     Khandhaka 9 At Campā
(Mv10.)     Khandhaka 10 At Kosambi

(Cullavagga)

(Cv1.)     Khandhaka 11 Saṅgha Procedures
(Cv2.)     Khandhaka 12 Probation
(Cv3.)     Khandhaka 13 Collected Procedures
(Cv4.)     Khandhaka 14 Settlement
(Cv5.)     Khandhaka 15 Minor Matters
(Cv6.)     Khandhaka 16 Lodgings
(Cv7.)     Khandhaka 17 Schism in the Saṅgha
(Cv8.)     Khandhaka 18 Duties
(Cv9.)     Khandhaka 19 Setting Aside the Sabbath
(Cv10.)     Khandhaka 20 Nuns
(Cv11.)     Khandhaka 21 Council of the Five Hundred
(Cv12.)     Khandhaka 22 Council of the Seven Hundred
